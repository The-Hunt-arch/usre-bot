Job Title: Machine Learning Engineer

Job Description: We are seeking a Machine Learning Engineer to join our AI team. In this role, you will work on a variety of ML and AI projects, and your primary responsibility will be to assist in the development and deployment of machine learning models. Whether you're a recent graduate with a Bachelor's degree or have a Master's degree in a relevant field, you'll have the opportunity to learn and grow while contributing to the development of cutting-edge AI solutions.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with ML/AI engineers on various projects.
  • Assist in data collection, preprocessing, and feature engineering.
  • Develop, test, and deploy machine learning models.
  • Perform model evaluation, optimization, and tuning.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in ML/AI technologies.
  • Contribute to documentation and knowledge sharing within the team.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Data Science, or a related field.
  • Strong understanding of machine learning fundamentals.
  • Proficiency in Python and relevant ML libraries (e.g., scikit-learn, TensorFlow, or PyTorch).
  • Knowledge of data manipulation and analysis tools (e.g., Pandas, NumPy).
  • Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git).
  • Familiarity with web frameworks like Django and Flask.
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
  • Strong communication and teamwork abilities.

Additional Preferred Skills:

  • Experience with deep learning models and frameworks.
  • Familiarity with cloud computing platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • Basic knowledge of data visualization tools (e.g., Matplotlib or Seaborn).
  • Previous internships or projects related to ML/AI.
  • Any relevant certifications or coursework.
